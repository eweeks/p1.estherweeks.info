About Me

I'm a distance education student hoping to earn a Master's degree in Information Technology, with a concentration in digital media arts and instructional design. I did my undergrad in art and am hoping to further develop my skills in digital media. I'm really looking forward to this course and building on the skills I already have.

I've been working as a seasonal ranger & naturalist with the National Park Service and split my time between Wyoming and Colorado. I love being outdoors and spend a lot of time hiking, backpacking, fishing, and skiing. I also enjoy dance and traveling.
